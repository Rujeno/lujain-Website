هوية علامة أزياء فاخرة تُبنى من الصفر — حروف محكمة، لوحة ألوان مكبوحة، وثقة تُقرأ في كل حجم وعلى كل سطح.

The Brief

LA-CORVET was entering a competitive fashion market with no visual identity and a need to establish themselves as a premium player from day one. The challenge: create a brand that reads as luxury without looking like a copy of European labels — something with its own character and confidence.

The Approach

The direction was refined minimalism. We built around a lettermark that carries weight in any size, a typographic system that feels editorial, and a palette that walks the line between classic and contemporary. Every decision was made to feel intentional — nothing decorative for its own sake, everything earned.

The Outcome

A complete brand system: primary and secondary logo lockups, full color and typography guidelines, business card and stationery design, and a packaging concept. The brand launched with a clear visual voice that positioned LA-CORVET as a name worth noticing.
