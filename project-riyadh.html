بنك الرياض

Riyadh Bank

تصميم جدارية احتفالية لبنك الرياض — مزيج بصري يجمع بين أيقونات أفق الرياض ورسالة العيد بلوحة ألوان تعكس هوية البنك على مساحة معمارية واسعة.

Riyadh Bank needed a large-scale wall mural for an Eid campaign — a statement piece that would greet customers walking into a branch with celebration and civic pride. The design had to feel monumental but warm, institutional but welcoming, and unmistakably tied to Riyadh's identity.

The composition was built around Riyadh's most recognizable skyline — Kingdom Tower and Al-Faisaliah — rendered in a teal duotone that anchors the bank's brand color into an architectural narrative. The Eid greeting was set in Arabic calligraphy at scale, floating above the cityscape. The result feels like a window into the city's spirit, not a poster on a wall.

A fully executed large-format mural design — production files prepared for direct print at architectural scale, installed in a Riyadh Bank branch as a permanent seasonal feature. The piece delivers brand presence and emotional warmth simultaneously.
