Spert Cosmetics

Spert Cosmetics

هوية علامة تجميل محلية صُممت لتنافس الأسماء العالمية بثقة — من تصميم العبوة إلى قوالب وسائل التواصل الاجتماعي، منظومة بصرية متكاملة ومتسقة.

المخرجات

السنة
2024

Spert was launching as a new local cosmetics brand in a market crowded with international names. They needed a brand that could hold its own on shelf against established competitors — one that communicated quality, care, and femininity without looking generic.

The identity was built around softness and precision. A wordmark that's clean but not cold, a palette drawn from skin tones and botanicals, and packaging that feels tactile even on a screen. The goal was something that a customer would pick up not because she had to, but because it felt right.

Full brand identity including logo, color and typography system, product label design, packaging templates, and a complete social media design kit. Spert launched with a cohesive brand presence that made it look like it had been around for years.
